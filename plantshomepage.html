Brynn's Official Plant Parent Guide

What Am I?

Brynn's official plant guide is a website created by a plant lover for any type of indoor plant parent. Whether you're an expert or a plant serial killer, this website will help you pick out the perfect plant. Take the quiz to determine what kind of plant parent you are! Then, head over to the web page designed for people just like you! Here you will find which plants you should buy and from where, along with basic care informaton. Happy planting!
